California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

California Residents: This page explains your privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and how to exercise them.

Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  1. Right to Know: You have the right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  2. Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  3. Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information (Note: ZestFlow AI does not sell personal information).
  4. Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your CCPA rights.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

Category Examples Business Purpose
Identifiers Name, email address, phone number, IP address Account creation, customer support, service delivery
Commercial Information Service usage, transaction history, preferences Service improvement, billing, analytics
Internet Activity Browser type, pages visited, interaction with services Website optimization, security, analytics
Geolocation Data IP-based location, service area Service availability, compliance
Professional Information Business name, industry type, business size Service customization, support

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following business purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Processing transactions and billing
  • Communicating with you about your account
  • Improving and developing new features
  • Detecting and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Marketing our services (with your consent)

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate our business (e.g., payment processors, SMS providers)
  • Business Partners: Integration partners with your explicit consent
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Your Privacy Choices

Opt-Out of Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email or by contacting us at privacy@zestflowai.com.

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block or delete cookies
  • Enable "Do Not Track" signals
  • Control location sharing

Mobile App Controls

Your device settings allow you to:

  • Control app permissions
  • Limit ad tracking
  • Manage location services

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your CCPA rights, you can:

Submit a Privacy Request

California residents can make up to two requests per year

Submit Privacy Request

Or call toll-free: 1-800-PRIVACY

Email: privacy@zestflowai.com

Verification Process

To protect your privacy and security, we will verify your identity before processing your request. We may ask for:

  • Account information (email, phone number)
  • Transaction details
  • Other information to confirm your identity

Response Timeline

  • We will confirm receipt within 10 business days
  • We will respond to your request within 45 days
  • If more time is needed, we'll notify you of the extension (up to 90 days total)

Authorized Agents

You may designate an authorized agent to make requests on your behalf. The agent must provide:

  • Written permission from you
  • Proof of their identity
  • Direct confirmation from you (we may contact you directly)

California "Shine the Light" Law

California residents may request information about disclosure of personal information to third parties for direct marketing purposes. Since we do not share personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes, this law does not apply to our practices.

Updates to This Notice

We may update this CCPA notice periodically. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top
  • Sending notice to your registered email
  • Posting a prominent notice on our website

Additional California Privacy Rights

Financial Incentives

We may offer financial incentives for providing personal information (such as discounts for email signup). Participation is voluntary, and you can withdraw at any time.

Minor's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors under 16 without parental consent. California minors may request removal of posted content by contacting us.
