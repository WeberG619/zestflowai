General Questions

What types of businesses does this work for? +

Perfect for any service business that gets emergency or after-hours calls:

  • Plumbers
  • HVAC technicians
  • Electricians
  • Locksmiths
  • Garage door repair
  • Restoration services
  • Appliance repair

If customers need you after hours, this system will capture them before they call your competitor.

Will this work with my current site? +

Yes! We integrate with any website platform:

  • Squarespace
  • WordPress
  • Wix
  • Custom HTML sites
  • Any platform with a contact form

We connect to your existing form - no website changes needed.

Do I need new software? +

No new software required. The system uses:

  • SMS (text messages to your phone)
  • Your existing calendar (Google, Outlook, etc.)
  • Optional: Google Sheets for lead tracking

Everything works through tools you already use daily.

What if it doesn't book a job? +

We remove it. No charge.

If you don't get at least one booked job in the first 7 days, we'll uninstall everything and refund your setup fee. No questions asked.

We only succeed when you book jobs. That's why we're confident in our guarantee.

Technical Setup Questions

Do I need to change my phone number? +

No! You have two options:

  • Keep your current number: Use call forwarding after hours
  • Dedicated line: Set up a new after-hours number

Most contractors forward their existing number after hours - it takes 30 seconds to set up.

How long does installation take? +

48 hours from start to finish:

  • Day 1: Technical setup (2-3 hours)
  • Day 2: Testing and training (1 hour)
  • Day 3: You're live and booking jobs!

We handle all the technical work. You just need to be available for a 30-minute training call.

What if I'm already booked when someone tries to schedule? +

Your calendar shows only available time slots. The system:

  • Syncs with your calendar in real-time
  • Only shows times you've marked as available
  • Blocks out times when you're already booked
  • Respects your business hours and days off

You stay in complete control of your schedule.

What about spam? +

We filter obvious spam automatically. The system:

  • Blocks known spam numbers
  • Filters nonsense messages
  • Requires valid phone numbers
  • Flags suspicious patterns

Odd messages trigger human handoff - you decide how to respond.

Pricing & Billing Questions

When do I start paying the monthly fee? +

Only after the system books your first job.

You pay the one-time setup fee ($499 or $699), then nothing else until we prove it works by booking an actual job.

This usually happens within the first week, but we don't charge monthly fees until it does.

Are there any hidden fees? +

No hidden fees. You only pay:

  • Setup fee (one-time)
  • Monthly service fee (after first job)
  • Standard SMS costs (about 1¢ per text)
  • Payment processing if you take deposits (2.9% + 30¢)

That's it. No surprises, no contracts, no cancellation fees.

Can customers pay the deposit with a credit card? +

Yes! With the Pro plan, we integrate with:

  • Stripe
  • Square
  • Your existing payment processor

Deposits go straight to your account minus standard processing fees. Customers get a receipt, you get paid upfront.

What if I want to cancel? +

Cancel anytime with no penalties:

  • We stop billing immediately
  • No contracts or termination fees
  • You keep all your lead data

We believe in earning your business every month.
