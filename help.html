Getting Started

Welcome to ZestFlow AI

Your after-hours booking system is designed to capture emergency service requests when you're not available. Here's everything you need to know to get started.

How It Works

  1. Customer contacts you - Via your website form or by calling/texting your phone
  2. Instant response - Our AI responds within 10-60 seconds
  3. Qualification - System asks about the emergency and location
  4. Booking - Customer selects available time from your calendar
  5. Confirmation - Both you and customer get confirmation

Pro Tip: Test the system yourself by calling your after-hours number from a different phone. This helps you understand the customer experience.

Initial Setup Checklist

During your 48-hour installation, we handle these items:

  • ✓ Connect your website form to our system
  • ✓ Set up Twilio phone number (or connect existing)
  • ✓ Integrate your Google Calendar
  • ✓ Configure SMS templates with your business info
  • ✓ Set up Google Sheets for lead tracking
  • ✓ Configure payment processing (if using Pro plan)
  • ✓ Test everything thoroughly
  • ✓ Provide 30-minute training session

System Configuration

Setting Business Hours

Define when the automated system should activate:

  1. Log into your dashboard
  2. Navigate to Settings → Business Hours
  3. Set your regular hours for each day
  4. The system automatically handles calls outside these hours

Important: Remember to update hours for holidays or when you're on vacation to avoid missed opportunities.

Customizing SMS Templates

Make responses sound like your business:

Available Templates

  • Initial Response - First message after contact
  • Qualification Questions - Gathering emergency details
  • Booking Confirmation - After appointment scheduled
  • Reminder Messages - Before appointment time

Template Variables

{customer_name} - Customer's name {business_name} - Your business name {appointment_time} - Scheduled time {service_type} - Type of service requested {deposit_amount} - If deposits enabled

Calendar Integration

Your calendar controls availability:

  • Only shows times you've marked as available
  • Automatically updates when you book other appointments
  • Respects buffer times between appointments
  • Can set different availability for emergency vs regular service

Managing Bookings

Handling New Bookings

When a customer books through the system:

  1. Immediate notification - You get SMS + email
  2. Calendar entry - Automatically added with all details
  3. Google Sheets log - New row with customer info
  4. Customer confirmation - They receive booking details

Best Practice: Always confirm emergency bookings with a personal call 30 minutes before arrival.

Managing Deposits (Pro Plan)

If you're collecting deposits:

  • Customer pays before booking is confirmed
  • Funds go directly to your Stripe/Square account
  • Standard processing fees apply (2.9% + 30¢)
  • Refunds handled through your payment processor

Setting Deposit Amounts

Configure different deposits for different services:

  • Fixed amount (e.g., $50 for all services)
  • Percentage of estimated job (e.g., 25%)
  • Service-specific (e.g., $75 for plumbing, $100 for HVAC)

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

System not responding to texts

  • Check if phone forwarding is active
  • Verify Twilio account has credits
  • Ensure system is outside business hours
  • Test with a different phone number

Calendar not showing correct availability

  • Refresh calendar permissions
  • Check for conflicting events
  • Verify time zone settings
  • Clear calendar cache

Not receiving notifications

  • Check spam folder for emails
  • Verify phone number is correct
  • Ensure notification settings are enabled
  • Test with manual booking

Error Messages

"Calendar sync failed" - Re-authorize Google Calendar access "Payment processing error" - Check Stripe/Square connection "SMS delivery failed" - Verify phone number format "Booking conflict" - Calendar shows double-booking

Best Practices

Maximizing Bookings

Response Time Matters

  • Our system responds in 10-60 seconds
  • Customers usually call 3-5 contractors
  • First response typically wins the job

Availability Strategy

  • Offer at least 2-3 time slots for emergencies
  • Keep some same-night availability
  • Update calendar immediately after manual bookings
  • Block out time for existing commitments

SMS Template Tips

  • Keep messages short and clear
  • Include your business name in first message
  • Emphasize quick response time
  • Make booking link prominent

Customer Experience

Remember: Customers in emergencies are stressed. Make the process as simple as possible.

  • Use empathetic language in templates
  • Provide clear pricing expectations
  • Send confirmation immediately
  • Follow up personally before arrival

Billing & Account

Understanding Your Bill

What You're Charged

  • Setup Fee: One-time charge ($499 or $699)
  • Monthly Fee: Begins after first booked job ($149 or $199)
  • SMS Costs: ~$0.01 per message (billed separately)
  • No hidden fees - That's everything

7-Day Guarantee

If the system doesn't book at least one job in your first 7 days, we remove it and refund your setup fee. No questions asked.
