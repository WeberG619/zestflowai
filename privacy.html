1. Overview

ZestFlow AI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our After-Hours Job Booking System.

Key Point: We believe in transparency and minimal data collection. We only collect what's necessary to provide our service, and you own your customer data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, business name, email address, phone number
  • Business Information: Service types, pricing, availability
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method (processed by Stripe)
  • Configuration Data: SMS templates, calendar settings, business hours

2.2 Information From Your Customers

When customers interact with your system, we process:

  • Customer names and phone numbers
  • Service requests and messages
  • Appointment details
  • Payment information (if deposits are enabled)

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third parties including:

  • Google: Calendar integration

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

5. Data Security

Our Security Commitment: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5.1 Technical Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity

5.2 Organizational Measures

  • Limited access to personal information
  • Employee confidentiality agreements
  • Regular security training
  • Vendor security assessments

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Closed Account Data: Deleted within 90 days (except as legally required)
  • SMS Records: 7 years for TCPA compliance
  • Payment Records: 7 years for tax compliance
  • Support Tickets: 2 years

7. Your Rights

7.1 Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your information
  • Port: Receive your data in a portable format

7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@zestflowai.com. We will respond within 30 days.
