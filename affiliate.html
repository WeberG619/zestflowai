Frequently Asked Questions

When do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly via PayPal or direct deposit. Payments are sent on the 15th of each month for the previous month's earnings. Minimum payout is $100.

How long do I earn commissions?

You earn 30% recurring commission for as long as your referral remains a paying customer. There are no time limits or commission caps.

Can I promote on Google Ads or Facebook?

Yes! You can promote ZestFlow AI through paid advertising. We just ask that you don't bid on our brand terms or create misleading ads.

Do you provide training?

Absolutely! New affiliates get access to our training portal, weekly Q&A calls, and a private Facebook group with 500+ successful affiliates.

What counts as a qualified referral?

A qualified referral is a contractor who signs up through your link, completes their trial, and becomes a paying customer. You earn commission from their first payment.
